Moisture Damage Prevention

Moisture Damage Prevention focuses on controlling humidity levels and minimizing condensation within packaging and transportation environments.

Effective prevention strategies protect products from corrosion, mold growth, deformation, and performance degradation during transit.

VTC pioneered calcium chloride–based, starch-supported in-box desiccants, redefining moisture protection within packaging systems.

Compared to silica gel and clay, VTC desiccants deliver significantly enhanced performance during extended transport cycles.

With high moisture absorption capacity, VTC desiccants ensure long-lasting protection for intercontinental shipments and demanding storage environments.

Early Protection Minimizes Long-Term Risk

Effective moisture protection requires integrating desiccant use into the entire supply chain. Moisture control should be part of a company’s quality management system, covering all stages from material handling to packaging and shipment, supported by clear processes and housekeeping practices.

Pre- and post-implementation support helps identify potential sources of moisture damage and raises awareness across teams. By highlighting risk points and providing practical guidance, moisture-related issues can be reduced at every operational level.

As a provider of moisture damage prevention solutions, we support customers throughout their operations with reliable products, technical expertise, and ongoing guidance to help minimize moisture risks and protect product quality.
