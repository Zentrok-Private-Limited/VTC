In-Box Desiccants

VIMS Techno Crafts in-box desiccants are specially engineered for use in transport and product packaging applications such as cartons, boxes, and poly bags.

These in-box desiccants are ideal for protecting hygroscopic products— including garments, wood, leather goods, and other moisture-sensitive items— as well as cargo vulnerable to corrosion. They effectively regulate humidity levels within product packaging.

Moisture inside packaging can originate from multiple sources: natural moisture released by the products themselves, moisture present in packaging materials such as cardboard cartons or wooden crates, or humidity that diffuses through packaging during storage and transit.

By controlling internal humidity, VIMS Techno Crafts ensures that your products reach their destination in a safe, dry, and damage-free condition.

Our Products

VIMS Techno Crafts in-box desiccants are available in multiple weight options ranging from 2g to 100g, suitable for a wide variety of packaging sizes and applications.
