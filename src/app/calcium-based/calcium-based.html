Calcium Based Desiccants

Calcium-Based Desiccants are high-performance moisture absorbers made primarily from calcium chloride (CaClâ‚‚). They work by absorbing moisture from the air and converting it into a liquid or gel form, making them highly effective in high-humidity environments. Compared to traditional silica gel, calcium-based desiccants offer much higher moisture absorption capacity. They are commonly used in in-box packaging for electronics, pharmaceuticals, garments, food packaging, and other moisture-sensitive products to protect them from humidity damage.

One of the key advantages of calcium-based desiccants is their ability to provide long-lasting moisture control, even during sea freight, long-term storage, and humid climate conditions. These desiccants are designed with leak-proof packaging to safely contain absorbed moisture. Because of their strong and reliable performance, they are widely used in logistics, export packaging, and industrial applications to prevent corrosion, mold growth, and product deterioration.
