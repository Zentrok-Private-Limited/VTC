Calcium-Based Desiccants are high-performance moisture absorbers made primarily from calcium chloride (CaClâ‚‚). They absorb moisture from the air and convert it into a liquid or gel form, making them highly effective in high-humidity environments. Compared to traditional silica gel, calcium-based desiccants offer much higher moisture absorption capacity.

These desiccants are widely used in in-box packaging for electronics, pharmaceuticals, garments, food packaging, and other moisture-sensitive products to protect them from humidity-related damage.

One of the key advantages is long-lasting moisture control, even during sea freight, long-term storage, and humid climate conditions. Designed with leak-proof packaging, Incubic desiccants safely contain absorbed moisture and help prevent corrosion, mold growth, and product deterioration in logistics and industrial applications.
