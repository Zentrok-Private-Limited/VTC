Industry Solutions

Garments & Textiles

Garments and textiles are highly sensitive to moisture, which can cause mold growth, odor, discoloration, and fabric degradation during storage and transit. Effective moisture control helps preserve fabric quality, appearance, and usability throughout the supply chain.

Leather Articles

Leather products are vulnerable to humidity-related damage such as mold, stiffness, cracking, and surface deterioration. Controlled moisture protection ensures durability, finish quality, and long-term value of leather goods.

Footwear

Excess moisture in footwear packaging can lead to odor, fungal growth, deformation, and material damage. Moisture control solutions help maintain hygiene, structure, and product performance during transportation.

Furniture

Wooden and upholstered furniture is prone to warping, swelling, corrosion, and mold when exposed to humidity. Proper moisture management protects structural integrity and surface finishes.

Handicrafts & Kitchen Utensils

Handicrafts and kitchen utensils made from wood, metal, or mixed materials are susceptible to corrosion, discoloration, and mold. Moisture control preserves craftsmanship, appearance, and functionality.

Machinery, Electrical & Electronic Goods

Machinery and electronic components are highly sensitive to moisture, which can cause corrosion, short circuits, and malfunction. Reliable moisture protection ensures operational safety and performance integrity.

Metal Products

Metal products are prone to rust and oxidation when exposed to humidity. Moisture control solutions reduce corrosion risks and help maintain surface quality during storage and transportation.

Agricultural Products

Agricultural goods require controlled humidity to prevent spoilage, mold, and quality loss. Effective moisture management helps extend shelf life and maintain freshness during transit and storage.
