About Us

VIMS TECHNO CRAFTS (VTC)

VTC empowers businesses to safeguard their goods against moisture-related damage during transportation and storage. Our solutions help preserve product quality, maintain value, and significantly reduce losses and waste.

Since its inception, VTC has been committed to becoming a trusted leader in moisture damage prevention, driving innovation in advanced desiccant technologies and solutions tailored for modern supply chains.

We pioneered the introduction of calcium chloride–based, starch-supported in-box desiccants, redefining how moisture protection is applied within packaging. Through advanced engineering, high-performance packaging materials, and newly developed sealing technologies, VTC created a highly efficient moisture-absorbing solution that can be placed in direct contact with goods vulnerable to moisture damage—an application previously limited to conventional and less effective desiccants such as silica gel or clay.

By continuously advancing our products, materials, and manufacturing processes, VTC drives progress within the industry. Our focus remains on delivering superior cargo protection solutions while optimizing usage through intelligent product design—ensuring maximum performance with minimal material application.
