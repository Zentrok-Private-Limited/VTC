About Us

VIMS TECHNO CRAFTS (VTC)

VIMS Techno Crafts (VTC) is a trusted provider of advanced moisture protection solutions, helping businesses safeguard their products during storage and transportation. Our solutions are designed to preserve product quality, protect packaging integrity, and minimize losses caused by humidity and environmental exposure.

Since its inception, VTC has focused on building reliable and efficient desiccant solutions tailored to the evolving needs of global supply chains. Through continuous research, quality-driven manufacturing, and practical design, we support industries in maintaining consistency and reliability across their packaging operations.

Our product portfolio includes a wide range of moisture control solutions engineered to perform under diverse climatic and logistical conditions. Each product is developed with a strong emphasis on performance, safety, and ease of use, ensuring seamless integration into existing packaging systems.

By combining technical expertise with a customer-focused approach, VTC delivers dependable moisture protection solutions that help businesses reduce risk, extend product shelf life, and maintain brand value across domestic and international markets.
