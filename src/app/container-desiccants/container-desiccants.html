Container Desiccants

Container desiccants are advanced moisture control solutions specifically designed for use inside shipping containers during long-distance transportation and extended storage periods. They play a critical role in protecting cargo from excessive humidity and temperature fluctuations that occur during sea and land transit. By effectively absorbing moisture from the air, container desiccants help prevent condensation buildup, commonly known as container rain, which can severely damage goods.

This moisture control is essential to avoid issues such as corrosion of metal parts, mold and mildew growth, degradation of packaging materials, and overall product quality loss. Container desiccants are widely used across industries including chemicals, electronics, machinery, textiles, pharmaceuticals, and food-grade cargo. Their reliable performance ensures cargo arrives at its destination in safe, dry, and damage-free condition, reducing losses and improving supply chain efficiency.

What Are Container Desiccants?

Container desiccants are high-absorption moisture control products installed inside containers, trailers, or enclosed cargo spaces. They continuously absorb excess humidity from the air, maintaining a dry environment throughout the shipping journey.

They are widely used in sea freight, rail transport, and long-term storage, especially where temperature fluctuations are frequent.

Key Features
